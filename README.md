# IMNCI — Integrated Management of Neonatal and Childhood Illness

Reveal.js slides for the undergraduate Community Medicine lecture on IMNCI (45 min, MBBS).

- Live slides: https://drarunmitra.github.io/imnci-lecture/

Covers the rationale and IMCI to IMNCI evolution, the three strategy components,
the colour-coded case-management process (assess, classify, treat, counsel, follow-up),
general danger signs and the four main symptoms, the sick young infant (PSBI), and
IMNCI delivery in India (ASHA/ANM/AWW, F-IMNCI). Designed to follow the two-part
Malnutrition lecture series, with cross-links to the SAM referral (NRC / CMAM / POSHAN 2.0)
pathways.
